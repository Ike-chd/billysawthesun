About The Artist

Melancholy,
warmth, and
honest storytelling.

Born and raised in Johannesburg, South Africa, Owami Mkhize — known professionally as BillySawTheSun — creates music that feels like a memory you can dance to.

Inspired heavily by the emotional vulnerability of Dominic Fike and the colorful indie textures of Rex Orange County, his sound blends alternative pop, lo-fi grooves, mellow guitars, and introspective lyricism into something deeply personal.

BillySawTheSun’s music lives somewhere between late-night drives, rooftop conversations, and the quiet moments people rarely talk about. Every release aims to feel cinematic without losing the raw honesty of a bedroom recording.
